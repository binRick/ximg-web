Common Design Flow

  1. Schematic capture (KiCad, EasyEDA, Altium)
  2. BOM & component footprint assignment
  3. Board outline definition
  4. Component placement (tall components away from connectors)
  5. Power/ground plane assignment
  6. Route critical signals first (clocks, diff pairs, RF)
  7. Route power traces (wider)
  8. Route signal traces
  9. DRC (Design Rule Check)
  10. Gerber export → fabrication

Key Rules of Thumb:
• Decouple every IC with a 100nF ceramic cap as close to Vcc pin as possible
• 45° or curved traces only (no 90° corners — RF rule, also aids manufacturability)
• Differential pairs: keep length-matched within 10 mils
• Keep analog and digital grounds separated, joined at a single point
• Wide copper pour for thermal relief on power devices
• Add fiducial marks for pick-and-place machines on production boards
