UAP/D

Unidentified Aerial Phenomenon, Detected

Top-down stealth UFO game with isometric 3D rendering. Abduct livestock. Avoid the witness threshold. Don't get classified.

C + raylib macOS iOS single-file

Mission Briefing

A rural farmstead. A stretch of I-95. The herd is unaware. You are not.

Drift in low. Hold the tractor beam over a cow until it lifts; a moment too long and the energy bar empties. A civilian inside your vision cone passes through Idle → Curious → Alarmed → Reporting; each tier feeds the threat meter faster. Hit 100% threat and the mission is classified. Lift every cow on the map and the level advances — civilian count scales up, the map flips between RENDLESHAM and I-95, and the herd gets larger.

Three flyable saucers, each with its own beam-energy economy. Hold C to lay procedural crop circles for the next morning's tabloids.

Features

🛸 Three Saucers

Hornet, Starlight, Greyhound — each with distinct speed, beam range, beam time, and energy economy.

👁 Vision-Cone AI

Four-state civilian alert: Idle → Curious → Alarmed → Reporting. Each tier escalates the threat feed rate.

🚔 Mixed Witnesses

FBI agents on foot and police cruisers (faster, slower-turning) split the spotter pool.

🚁 Patrol Helicopters

Search beams sweep the airspace. On detect they lock and chase for 10s; ignore watchtowers, rain threat at 16/s.

🌾 Crop Circles

Hold C to trace concentric rings, 8-fold petals, six-arm spirals. Get spotted mid-lay and the pattern dies.

🎞 Witness Polaroids

Vintage UFO photographs slide in from the screen edge whenever a civilian first spots you.

🎵 Reactive Audio

Looped title/beam/ambient music that ducks the moment any civilian sees you. One-shot stings for spotted/escaped/fly-by.

🗺 Live Minimap

Cow targets, all enemies (color-coded by alert state), the UFO, and the camera viewport — all live in the top-left.

🌀 Endless Levels

Each clear scales civilian count and flips the map. Run mode (Shift) burns through engine-startup stamina.

Tech

LanguageSingle-file C, ~3.9k lines in src/main.c
Engineraylib 5.5 (no abstraction layer)
RenderingTrue 3D scene, orthographic isometric Camera3D
ModelsglTF (.glb) loaded once, NormalizeModel pre-bakes a transform per model
Mapfarm.glb (272 MB) pre-rendered to PNG cache; iOS bundle ships only the PNG
iOSUIKit + CAEAGLLayer + CADisplayLink bridge driving StepFrame()
Build./run.sh produces a real macOS .app bundle with Cmd+Tab icon
