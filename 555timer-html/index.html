Formulat = 1.1 × R × C

Output Pulse

Monostable Operation:
Triggered by a LOW pulse on pin 2 (TRIGGER). Output goes HIGH for t = 1.1×R×C seconds, then returns LOW. Useful for debouncing, pulse stretching, and time delays.
