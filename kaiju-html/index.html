KAIJU

Top-down isometric rampage

You are the monster. Eat the city before the military puts you down. A role-reversal disaster movie — sister project of UAP/D, same iso-3D renderer, opposite power fantasy.

C + raylib macOS iOS-planned single-file

Core Loop

Wreck → grow → wave escalates → wreck harder. 8–15 minute runs.

You start the size of a small office building. Eating civilians, cars, power pylons, and entire skyscrapers feeds a MASS meter. Each time it fills you visibly scale up — the camera pulls back, your melee reach lengthens, your atomic breath travels further, your turn rate slows. By wave 5 you're chewing the spire off a finance tower.

The military responds in proportion to MEDIA — a global threat stat driven by destruction, civilian deaths, and the news helicopters that keep painting you for broadcast.

Wave Escalation

WaveThreat
1Panicked civilians, police, news vans (free MASS)
2SWAT trucks, attack helicopters arrive — first real fire
3Tanks, APCs, artillery emplacements at city edge
4F-22 strafing runs — duck behind skyscrapers or eat the missiles
5BOSS — JAEGER MK-I — a piloted mech your size, melee duel
6Railgun batteries, EMP drones (stuns breath for 4s)
7Naval bombardment — battleships in the bay lob shells
8Tactical strike package — cluster bombs, danger-close artillery
9BOSS — RIVAL KAIJU — equal-size monster, you both keep wrecking the map
10+Nuke delivery — B-2 paints a 400-unit blast zone; flee in 12s or eat the warhead

Endless after wave 10; second rival kaiju every 5 waves.

Destruction Model

Buildings

HP-gated. Hit threshold → animated collapse, debris cloud, dust shader, lingering rubble pile.

Power Pylons

Instant break; sparks recharge the atomic-breath meter.

Bridges

Snap in two. Vehicles on them ragdoll into the river.

Vehicles

Stomp = squashed sprite + oil spray. Throwing one acts as a ranged projectile that explodes on impact.

Eaten foundations

Buildings you've eaten leave craters. By end of a long run the skyline is visibly half gone.

Trees / signs

Cosmetic crunch — for flavor, not points.

Tech

LanguageSingle-file C in src/main.c
Engineraylib (no abstraction layer)
RenderingTrue 3D scene, orthographic isometric Camera3D
