Reading the spectrum

The x-axis is frequency (logarithmic scale, ~20 Hz left to 20 kHz right). Bar height = amplitude at that frequency. The fundamental is the tallest bar; harmonics appear at integer multiples. A sine wave shows only one bar; a sawtooth has all harmonics (1/n roll-off); a square has odd harmonics only.
