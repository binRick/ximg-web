Key Formulas

R₀ = β / γ  —  Basic reproduction number (avg new infections per infected person)
Herd immunity threshold = 1 − 1/R₀  —  Fraction of population that must be immune to stop spread
Peak infection time ≈ ln(N·β/γ) / (β−γ)
