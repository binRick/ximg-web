Why impedance matching? Maximum power transfer occurs when source and load impedances are complex conjugates (Z_source = Z_load*). Mismatches cause reflections — characterized by VSWR (Voltage Standing Wave Ratio). In RF systems, a 2:1 VSWR means ~11% reflected power. Transformers match at DC/low-frequency; L/T/π networks match at RF.
