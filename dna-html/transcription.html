Double Helix Replication Transcription Translation

Transcription — DNA to mRNA

RNA polymerase reads the template strand (3'→5') and synthesizes mRNA (5'→3').

Initiation
Stage: Initiation

RNA polymerase binds to the promoter sequence (TATA box) upstream of the gene. The transcription bubble opens ~12-17 base pairs around the active site.

RNA vs DNA

In mRNA, Uracil (U) replaces Thymine (T). RNA is single-stranded. RNA polymerase does not need a primer unlike DNA polymerase.

Three Stages

Initiation: RNAP binds promoter.
Elongation: RNAP moves 3'→5' along template, builds mRNA 5'→3'.
Termination: RNAP reaches terminator sequence, mRNA released.
