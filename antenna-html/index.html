Impedance~36 Ω (≥32 radials: ~50 Ω)
Gain~5.2 dBi over perfect ground
Quarter-wave verticals require a ground plane (radials) for proper operation. With 4 radials: ~36Ω. With 32+ radials: approaches 50Ω (direct coax match). Radials can be elevated (reduces required count) or buried. 60° angle elevated radials = best compromise.
