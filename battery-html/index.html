Safe Handling

Never puncture, crush, or short-circuit a LiPo cell. Always use a LiPo-specific balance charger. Store at 3.8V/cell (~50% SoC) for long-term storage. Never leave charging unattended. Puffed (swollen) cells should be safely discharged and disposed of — do not continue using. Always charge in a fireproof LiPo bag.

Configuration Rules

Series (xS) — adds voltage, capacity stays the same. All cells must be same capacity and charged equally.

Parallel (xP) — adds capacity (mAh), voltage stays the same. Cells must be at same voltage before connecting.

Series-Parallel (xSxP) — combine both. A 3S2P pack from 3.7V/3000mAh cells gives 11.1V / 6000mAh / 66.6Wh.
