Kirchhoff's Laws

KCL — Current Law

ΣI_in = ΣI_out

The sum of currents entering a node equals the sum of currents leaving it. Current is conserved — charge cannot accumulate at a node.

KVL — Voltage Law

ΣV = 0 around any loop

The sum of all voltages around any closed loop is zero. Energy is conserved — what goes up must come down.
