GLP-1 Optimization Peptides
💉
Tirzepatide (GIP/GLP-1 Dual Agonist)
2.5 mg SC injection · Weekly · Compounded
Dual Agonist
Activates both GLP-1 and GIP receptors simultaneously, producing superior weight loss and glycemic control vs. GLP-1 monotherapy alone. Clinical trials (SURMOUNT-1) demonstrated up to 22.5% body weight reduction. Currently being evaluated as a step-up from semaglutide if titration plateaus.
Why it's relevant for Alex
Current semaglutide plateau at 17 lbs. Tirzepatide's dual mechanism may overcome GLP-1 receptor desensitization and restore weight loss momentum. GIP component also improves lean mass preservation compared to GLP-1 alone.
GLP-1 / GIP Weight Loss Rx Required Glycemic Control
🧬
Retatrutide (GIP/GLP-1/Glucagon Triple Agonist)
Phase III — Not yet approved · Monitoring pipeline
Pipeline
Triple receptor agonist adding glucagon receptor activation to GIP+GLP-1 signaling. Phase II data showed up to 24.2% mean weight reduction — the highest of any obesity pharmacotherapy in trials. Glucagon component boosts resting metabolic rate and fat oxidation beyond GLP-1 alone.
Future consideration
If Alex's metabolic rate adaptation reduces efficacy of current protocol, retatrutide's thermogenic glucagon component directly addresses adaptive thermogenesis — the main reason GLP-1 weight loss stalls at 12–18 months.
Research Stage Triple Agonist Thermogenic
Metabolic Health Peptides
⚡
AOD-9604
300 mcg SC injection · Daily, 5 days on / 2 off
Fat Metabolism
Modified fragment (176–191) of human growth hormone. Stimulates lipolysis (fat breakdown) and inhibits lipogenesis (new fat formation) without the insulin resistance or IGF-1 elevation of full HGH. Particularly effective for visceral and truncal fat — Alex's primary adiposity pattern.
Target: Visceral fat reduction
Current protocol: 8-week cycle. Pairs synergistically with semaglutide — GLP-1 reduces caloric intake while AOD-9604 directly accelerates lipolysis from adipocytes. No impact on blood glucose.
HGH Fragment Lipolysis 8-wk Cycle No IGF-1 effect
🔋
MOTS-c
10 mg SC injection · 3× per week
Mitochondrial
Mitochondria-derived peptide that acts as a metabolic regulator. Activates AMPK, improves insulin sensitivity, and enhances skeletal muscle glucose uptake. Shown to reduce obesity and improve exercise capacity in pre-clinical and early human studies. Particularly relevant given Alex's T2D risk profile.
Target: Insulin sensitivity + mitochondrial function
Complements metformin's AMPK activation via a distinct mitochondrial pathway. May partially offset the metabolic slowdown seen during GLP-1-induced weight loss.
AMPK Activator Insulin Sensitivity Early Human Data 12-wk Cycle
🩺
Tesamorelin
1 mg SC injection · Daily (evening)
FDA Approved
GHRH analogue — FDA-approved for HIV-associated lipodystrophy; used off-label for visceral adiposity reduction. Stimulates pulsatile GH release, reduces visceral fat, improves triglycerides and IGF-1. One of the best-studied peptides for reducing central adiposity in metabolic syndrome.
Target: Visceral adiposity + lipid profile
Alex's baseline TG was 198 mg/dL. Tesamorelin's mechanism (GH-mediated lipolysis of visceral depots) is complementary to semaglutide and synergizes with resistance training for body composition improvement.
GHRH Analogue Visceral Fat Rx Required Lipid Improvement
Recovery & Muscle Preservation Peptides
🏋️
BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound)
250 mcg SC injection · Daily · 12-week cycle
Recovery
Gastric pentadecapeptide with potent systemic healing properties. Accelerates tendon, ligament, muscle, and joint recovery. Upregulates growth hormone receptors at injury sites. Particularly relevant during GLP-1 use when caloric deficit may slow tissue repair. Strong safety profile in pre-clinical studies.
Target: Injury prevention + workout recovery
Alex reported mild right knee discomfort during squats at week 12. BPC-157 is prioritized to support connective tissue integrity while training intensity increases. Also improves gut motility — a secondary benefit given semaglutide-related constipation.
Tissue Repair Tendon / Joint 12-wk Cycle Pre-clinical
💪
TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4 Fragment)
2.0 mg SC injection · 2× per week · First 4 weeks, then 1× / week maintenance
Recovery
Synthetic version of the naturally occurring thymosin beta-4 protein. Promotes actin polymerization, accelerates muscle repair, reduces inflammation at injury sites, and improves flexibility. Often stacked with BPC-157 for a comprehensive soft-tissue recovery protocol. Regenerative rather than anabolic.
Target: Muscle repair + anti-inflammation
BPC-157 + TB-500 stack is the standard integrative protocol for connective tissue support during aggressive weight loss programs. Preserves training quality and reduces injury risk when caloric intake is reduced by GLP-1.
Thymosin β-4 Anti-inflammatory BPC-157 Stack Muscle Repair
🌙
CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin Stack
CJC-1295 (DAC) 1 mg · 2× / week  |  Ipamorelin 200 mcg SC · Nightly at bedtime
GH Secretagogue
CJC-1295 is a long-acting GHRH analogue that amplifies growth hormone pulses; Ipamorelin is a selective GHRP that mimics ghrelin at the pituitary to stimulate GH release without cortisol or prolactin spikes. Together they produce sustained, physiologic GH pulses — peaking during slow-wave sleep — improving body composition, recovery speed, and lean mass retention.
Target: Lean mass preservation during GLP-1 weight loss
Critical for Alex: GLP-1-induced weight loss includes ~25–35% lean tissue unless aggressively countered. CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin at night elevates GH during the anabolic window, protecting muscle while semaglutide drives fat loss. Pairs with protein loading and resistance training.
